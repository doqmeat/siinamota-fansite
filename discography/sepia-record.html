my thoughts

i actually discovered siinamota through this album! spotify recomended me brown and stoy. and i just fell in love. i've listened to every siinamota record out there and i love every single one of them. but sepia record has something very special for me. it is a record that feels very ambient, melancholic, and yet very sweet. it has a bit of a heavy atmosphere with songs like the intro, stoy, brown, sky fish, and 999. i also find the over arching story of zinnia and meme so gorgeous (will write more about this eventually)

just a beautiful album to me...
