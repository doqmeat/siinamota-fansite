what is a fanlisting?

 a fanlisting is simply a listing of people who are fans of said subject... yes that is really it! you can join a fanlisting by applying to one with your name, country, and e-mail. it is basically a place where other fans can meet fans of the same subject from all over the world. you can find more info on fanlistings here.
