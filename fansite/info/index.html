fansite

this fansite started as a guide on notion as a way to compile info about siinamota's music because of the lack of wiki's related to him. with time, i've been getting more familiar with making websites and since then i wanted to host the fansite by myself and build it from the top!

in here, you will find pages dedicated for compiling siinamota's art, discography, interviews, album scans, and other things i've stumbled upon. i will also be listings my own siinamota related stuff like the fanlisting open for any siinamota fan to join!

overall, this site was built because i want to share this info with other fans and for my love for siinamota's music.

note: this fansite is a huge WIP while i move everything from notion to here. i am currently focusing on one page at a time.
